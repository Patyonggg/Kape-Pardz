Our Story

Born from a Love of the Perfect Cup.

KAPE PARDZ began as a scribbled idea on a napkin during a sourcing trip to the highlands. We realized that while specialty coffee was growing, the warmth of a true neighborhood hub was often lost in the pursuit of clinical perfection.


We spend weeks each year traveling to small, family-owned farms to hand-select the beans that make their way into your morning cup. But beyond the beans, this cafe is about the people. Nestled right here in San Juan, we built this space to be a refuge.

"We didn't open a coffee shop. We opened a living room for the neighborhood."

— Elena Reyes, Co-Founder
